🔐 Signing In

Click Sign in with Google using your @bu.edu Google account that has access to the shared inventory Google Drive. Hit allow for the email-sharing prompt (used for changelog tracking) and allow both Google Drive and Google Sheets permissions (used to edit the sheet and upload tank photos). Your session stays active until you sign out. Use Test Mode to explore without saving anything.

🧭 Navigation

Click the 🐟 FZFish logo in the top-left to open the nav menu:

  • 🐠 Inventory — the full tank list
  • 🔀 Breeding & Lineage — set up and track breeding crosses, and search any tank's ancestry
  • 🧪 Experiments — create and open named tank groupings
  • 📜 Recent Activity — a read-only feed of every change made through the app (who, what, when)
  • 📖 Help Guide — this guide, plus the DPF calculator

Only one section is shown at a time — switching to Inventory always shows the full, unfiltered list, even if you were inside an experiment.

➕ Adding a Tank

Click + Add Tank (desktop) or the + button (bottom-right on mobile). You may also scan a new barcode to begin adding.

  • Scan the barcode with 📷 (the barcode text is in the bottom-left of the label). Active tanks require a valid barcode ID — please attach barcodes as soon as possible.
  • No barcode yet? Click the Split button under Tank ID to auto-assign a temporary SPL-ID. A 3-day alert will remind you to add a real barcode.
  • Fill in the line name using A:b;C:D x Z formatting (e.g. dbh:eGFP;CD4:mCherry x CD8:eGFP).
  • The fertilization date must be a complete, valid MM/DD/YYYY date — incomplete or impossible dates (like Feb 30) are rejected on save.
  • If the Tank ID you enter is already in use, you'll be asked to either cancel and pick a different ID, or save anyway with a -1/-2/… suffix — this creates a 7-day alert reminding you to give it a proper unique ID.
  • Input locations using Rx Sy or Nx Sy where R/N = rack or nursery, S = shelf counting up from the ground.
  • In the Notes section, check 🔔 Remind me to remove mesh baffles in 7 days if this tank is coming out of the nursery and needs baffle removal.

🧬 Markers

Add markers using the dropdown pickers when editing a tank. Once added to any tank, a marker appears as a filter option across the app.

  • Positive markers (green tags) — transgenes confirmed present
  • Negative markers (red tags) — transgenes confirmed absent
  • Unsorted markers (gray tags) — transgenes not yet confirmed as present or absent; use these for fish whose genotype is still pending

🏷️ Status

Set the tank's current status when adding or editing. Statuses are also used to filter the inventory:

  • Active — Adult fish living on the rack.
  • Nursery — Fish in the nursery system.
  • Incubator — Embryos or early larvae currently in the incubator.
  • Breeding — Currently set up in a breeding cross.
  • Archived — No longer active; kept for record.

🏞️ Pictures

Please add photos at each stage — this includes embryo plates — to make the inventory useful for anyone working on your project. Photos are automatically saved to the shared Google Drive folder. Use the dot under the uploaded photo to adjust the thumbnail crop so the info tape is in frame.

📷 Barcode Scanning

Tap the 📷 icon in the header to scan a tank barcode. The app will jump straight to that tank or start a new tank entry. You can also scan while adding or editing a tank to fill in the Tank ID field automatically.

✏️ Editing, Duplicating & Deleting

Click any tank card to open the detail panel. From there you can:

  • Edit — modify all fields. Changes sync to the Google Sheet immediately.
  • Duplicate — create a new pre-filled tank with the same line, markers, status, and location. Tank ID, photo, and notes are cleared so you can fill them in fresh.
  • Delete from Inventory — permanently removes the tank. This can't be undone.
  • Click the Line name, Tank ID, or Location in the drawer to copy it to your clipboard (look for the ⎘ icon).

If you move a tank, update its status and location in the inventory. All changes are also saved to a Changelog tab on the sheet, viewable in-app under 🐟 logo → 📜 Recent Activity.

🔍 Searching & Filtering

Use the search bar to find tanks — by default, plain terms match line, Tank ID, and notes; open Fields ▾ (desktop only) to also match location, markers, or status with plain terms, or just use a loc: / marker: / status: prefix below, which always works. Use the status chips (Active, Nursery, Incubator…) to filter by status. Use the +Markers / −Markers / ?Markers dropdowns to filter by confirmed presence, confirmed absence, or unsorted transgenes. Click ⬇ Export to download whatever's currently visible as a CSV — this respects your active filters, and exports just that experiment's tanks if you're inside one. On mobile, tap ⚙ Filters to access all options.

Multi-term search: separate words are AND'd together — fli1 cd4 returns only tanks matching both.

Field prefixes (desktop): use field:value to target a specific field regardless of the Fields selector. Available prefixes:

  • line:fli1 — search line name
  • id:C12345678 — search tank ID
  • notes:spine — search notes
  • loc:R2A — search location
  • marker:eGFP — search any marker (+/−/?)
  • status:nursery — search status

Mix prefixes and plain terms freely: line:fli1 notes:uncertain

☑️ Selection Mode

Tap ☑ Select to enter selection mode. Click individual cards or shift-click to range-select a group. From the selection bar you can:

  • Add to Experiment — bulk-add selected tanks to any existing experiment via the dropdown.
  • Delete — bulk-delete selected tanks from the inventory. This can't be undone.

When inside an experiment, the Select button lets you pick tanks to remove from that experiment (without deleting from inventory).

🧪 Experiments

Open 🐟 logo → 🧪 Experiments to see the list of named experiments and create new ones. Experiments are collections of tanks stored as separate tabs in the same Google Sheet — useful for grouping tanks by project or cross. The list always reloads fresh from the sheet when you open the tab, and each experiment's tank grid loads fresh when you open it, so neither view can go stale.

  • Click + New Experiment to create one.
  • Click + Add Tanks to enter selection mode — existing members are pre-selected. Confirm to save the new set.
  • Click ⬤ Group to open a menu and add tanks to a color sub-group: choose a color (or "Remove from group"), then select the tanks to assign.
  • The colored dots on the bar next to ⬤ Group are a view filter — click one to highlight (show only) that color's tanks, click it again to show everyone. This doesn't change any tank's group.
  • Rename an experiment by clicking ✏️ in the experiment bar; click the name itself (not the pencil) to copy it.
  • Click 📅 in the experiment bar to give the experiment a date — whatever you want it to mean (start date, target date, etc.). It also shows next to the tank count in the experiments list.
  • Click 📝 in the experiment bar to add or edit free-form notes about the experiment. Notes save automatically when you click away from the text box, and the icon shows a dot when notes exist.
  • Click ⬇ Export in the filter bar to download just this experiment's tanks as a CSV.
  • Click Delete to permanently remove the experiment. This can't be undone.
  • Click ✕ Exit to return to the experiments list.
  • You can also add or remove a tank from any experiment directly from its detail drawer.

🔔 Alerts

FZFish automatically sets reminders for time-sensitive tasks. Click the 🔔 button in the header (or mobile toolbar) to open the alert panel.

  • Split / temp ID alert — triggered when you click the Split button under Tank ID. A 3-day reminder will fire to prompt you to replace the temporary SPL-ID with a real barcode. The alert auto-dismisses as soon as you save a valid barcode (C + 8 digits) for that tank.
  • Nursery ID alert — triggered silently when you save a Nursery tank without a valid barcode ID. A 3-day reminder will appear to prompt you to scan one in. Also auto-dismisses when a real barcode is saved.
  • Baffle removal alert — check 🔔 Remind me to remove mesh baffles in 7 days in the Notes section when saving a tank. A 7-day reminder will appear in the alert panel.
  • Breeding follow-up alert — created when you set up a cross in 🔀 Breeding & Lineage. Fires after the number of days you chose (default 7) and opens straight into the offspring recording flow.
  • Duplicate Tank ID alert — created when you choose to save a tank anyway with a numbered suffix after entering an ID that's already in use. A 7-day reminder will appear to prompt you to give it a proper unique ID. Auto-dismisses once you edit the tank to a unique ID.

From the alert panel you can Snooze any alert for 1 hour, or Dismiss it permanently. Deleting a tank also dismisses all its alerts.

Right after signing in, if anything needs attention you'll get a one-time toast broken down by type (e.g. "3 alerts: 2 nursery barcode needed, 1 cross follow-up") so you don't have to open the panel just to check.

Custom reminders: use + New reminder at the bottom of the alert panel to add your own — give it a title and a date/time and it shows up alongside the automatic alerts.

📅 Calendar Sync: every alert — automatic and custom — automatically gets a matching event on a shared FZFish Alerts calendar, titled with the email of whoever's account created it, with a popup reminder. Dismissing or snoozing an alert updates or removes its calendar event. This runs for whichever real account happens to be signed in — there's no personal switch to flip. Click 📅 Subscribe to Alerts at the top of the alert panel to add that shared calendar to your own Google Calendar (a one-time, read-only opt-in — it doesn't change any permissions). Needs a one-time setup by an admin first (see the README's "Setting up Google Calendar Sync" section) — until then, alerts just won't show up on the calendar.

🔀 Breeding & Lineage

Open 🐟 logo → 🔀 Breeding & Lineage — this is where you set up crosses, follow them through to offspring, and trace a tank's family tree.

Setting up a cross:

  • Click ＋ New Cross, then pick Parent 1 by scanning its barcode (📷) or choosing it from a searchable list (📋).
  • Toggle Incross to breed a single line against itself instead of picking a second parent.
  • Pick the fertilization date — Tomorrow (the stock default, since that's the day the eggs are actually fertilized), Today, or Custom for any other date. Fully editable later.
  • Set the follow-up reminder — how many days until you're prompted to record offspring. Fully editable, default 7.
  • Optionally attach setup photos of the breeding tank and add notes. This is just a snapshot of the day, not a permanent record — photos are automatically deleted 14 days after fertilization.
  • Both parent tanks are automatically switched to Breeding status so they show up together in the inventory.
  • Each cross card shows its Cross ID next to the setup date — click it to copy (⎘).

Recording offspring:

  • When the follow-up reminder fires, tap Record offspring from the alert or the cross card.
  • This opens the normal Add Tank form pre-filled with a suggested line name (Parent1 × Parent2, or Line inx. for an incross) — fully editable — a note linking back to the parents, and the fertilization date pre-filled from the cross's own date.
  • Use Save & Add Another to log as many resulting tanks as you need from one cross; the form stays open and resets between saves.
  • The cross is automatically marked completed once at least one offspring tank is recorded.

Managing a cross (from its card in the panel):

  • Edit — change either parent, the incross toggle, follow-up timing, notes, or photos at any time.
  • 📷 Photo — add more setup photos later.
  • Mark done — close out the cross without recording offspring.
  • Cancel — abandon the cross. This can't be undone; any setup photos are deleted.

Lineage: every tank's detail drawer has a 🧬 Lineage section showing what it was bred from, any crosses it's been a parent in with their recorded offspring, and an expandable multi-generation ancestry tree. Use 🔀 Start cross from this tank in the drawer to jump into New Cross with that tank pre-filled as Parent 1.

You can also set or change which cross a tank is offspring of directly from the Add/Edit Tank form — a 🧬 Lineage dropdown lets you link it to any existing cross (or unlink it), independent of the Record Offspring flow. Handy for correcting a tank's lineage after the fact.

⟳ Syncing

Click the ⟳ button to pull the latest data from the Google Sheet. The app syncs automatically on sign-in. All edits are written directly to the sheet — no manual export needed.

📲 Install as an App

FZFish can be installed directly on your phone or computer — no App Store needed. It opens full-screen like a native app.

iPhone / iPad (Safari):

  • Open the site in Safari (must be Safari, not Chrome).
  • Tap the Share button (the box with an arrow at the bottom of the screen).
  • Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  • Tap Add — FZFish will appear on your home screen like any app.

Android (Chrome):

  • Open the site in Chrome.
  • Tap the ⋮ menu (top-right) and tap Add to Home screen.
  • Or look for the Install app banner that appears automatically.

Desktop (Chrome / Edge):

  • Look for the install icon (⊕) in the address bar and click it.
  • Or open the browser menu and choose Install FZFish.
